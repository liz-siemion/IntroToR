Chapter 1 Overview

I reference course materials from USU’s ecology center workshop, and Dr. Simona Picardi’s Reproducible data science website.

1.1 Workshop Goals

This course is designed to teach the foundational programming concepts of R and how to use RStudio. Our goal is to equip you with the skills to continue learning R independently and to integrate R programming into your analyses and workflows.

Artwork by Allison Horst
